Welcome to your GroupMe Portal! Here's how to get started:
  1. Create GroupMe Bots: Go to GroupMe Developer Console and create bots for your group chats.
  2. Add Your Groups: Click "Add Group Chat" and enter your group details including the Bot ID.
  3. Create Posts: Upload posters, write messages, and schedule them to be sent automatically.
  4. Manage Schedule: View and manage all your scheduled posts from the dashboard.
